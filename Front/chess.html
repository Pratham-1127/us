Chess

In my childhood, I used to play chess in school. I remember we went to play district level chess competetion and I lost by 2 points. Since then I left playing chess.

All thanks to that one online friend who brought me back to this. I started playing it again and suddenly my interest in it started growing exponentially. Then I had like a year gap until I started playing it again.

Now, it's my daily dose of entertainment. I enjoy playing it.

As white, most of the times I start with typical E4 and as Black, I love to play Open Sicilian. I'm leaning forward to learn Carokann and Queen's Gambit in near future.
